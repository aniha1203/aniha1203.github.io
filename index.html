The Blue Lagoon

One place to visit in Iceland is The Blue Lagoon. It is beautiful!!! It is located in Southwestern Iceland, near Grindavik. It is a geothermal spa, in front of Mount Þorbjörn and an amazing place to go if you ever visit Iceland.
